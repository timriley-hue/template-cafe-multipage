After two years of squeezing past each other behind the counter, we finally have a kitchen we can turn around in.

The renovation took three weeks. We were closed for most of it, which was not ideal, but the result is worth it. We can now make twice the volume of food we could before, and the new oven means we can bake on-site rather than buying in.

The menu hasn't changed much — we're not doing anything fancy — but the soups, quiches, and cakes are all made here now. You'll notice the difference.

Thanks to everyone who messaged asking when we'd be back. We missed you too.
